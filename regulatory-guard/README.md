# Regulatory Guard

Audit a website or web app for strict compliance with applicable legislation across four domains:

- **Privacy** — GDPR, CCPA / CPRA, ePrivacy, PIPL, LGPD
- **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.2 AA, ADA Title III, European Accessibility Act (EAA), EN 301 549, Section 508
- **Consumer & E-commerce** — DSA, DMA, Consumer Rights / Omnibus / UCP Directives, CCPA "sale", CAN-SPAM
- **AI & Content** — EU AI Act, Copyright Directive, DMCA, state AI disclosure laws

## Why this skill exists

Public-facing apps inherit a long tail of legal obligations the moment they ship. This skill turns that tail into a structured, evidence-backed audit that an agent can run before launch, before entering a new market, or on a recurring cadence.

## What it produces

A tiered compliance report with:
- Applicability matrix (which laws apply, with reasoning)
- Findings grouped by domain, each with evidence, cited law, and remediation
- Severity: BLOCKER / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW / UNVERIFIED / PASS
- Concrete remediation plan for BLOCKER + HIGH

## What this skill is NOT

- Not legal advice. High-severity findings should go to qualified counsel.
- Not a guarantee. Compliance is contextual; this is a structured starting point.
- Not a replacement for a DPIA or formal accessibility audit by certified auditors when those are legally required.
